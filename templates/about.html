welcome to kalibre

how to use?

  • you can create an account and then go to "my emails", where you can add emails you'd like to send your books to.
  • click 'kalibre' in the upper-left corner to head back to the book search page
  • after finding a book, send it to the emails you've added in "my emails" by clicking the send button, please wait a second the process takes a few moments!

sending books straight to your kindle

  • go to your amazon account on the amazon website
  • head to 'Manage Your Content and Devices' page
  • head to 'Preferences' tab --> scroll down to 'Personal Document Settings'
  • find 'Approved Personal Document E-mail List' and add the email bllendev@gmail.com
  • thank you pine for your wonderful artwork!
  • thank you harrison-broadbent for libgen-api! - this app uses a modified version of this package
